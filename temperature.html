vs. Max Temperature


The graph above portrays a relationship between the latitude of a city and the max temperature of the city. Based off the graph, we can see an expected relationship between the latitude and the temperature of the city. The weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator which is the 0 degree latitude mark. Another thing to mention is that the cities in the southern hemisphere are warmer during this time of the year (January 5th, 2017) compared to the cities in the northern hemisphere. This is due to Earth's tilt on it's axis during this time of the year meaning it is warmer in the southern hemisphere compared to the northern hemisphere.
